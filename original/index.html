    About Us

    Let's get to know RPM further!

    We believes that getting to know your business partners deeply is the key to the success of a sustainable business chain.

    RPM really pays attention to the satisfaction of all its partners and customers, apart from providing quality products, we also ensure that our products are guaranteed in :

    • Product cleanliness.
    • Product halal.
    • An affordable price.
    • Stock availability.
    • Trust and commitment.

    In order to achieve all of this, we carry out our role based on the 5 SMART missions.

    S

    Suply Chain Optimization

    Develop and maintain a distribution network efficient and smart, ensuring every food product arrives on time and in the right place.

    M

    Maintain High Quality

    Guarantee that all our products distribute meets quality and safety standards highest.

    A

    Achieve Operational Excellence

    Implement best practices in operations for increase efficiency, productivity and customer satisfaction.

    R

    Reliable Partnership

    Building and strengthening relationships with farmers, producers, and business partners to create an ecosystem mutual support and benefit.

    T

    Trustworthy Sustainability

    Committed to sustainable business practices and provide a positive impact on the community and environment.

    Services

    RPM have various services and facilities to support its role in supporting customers' needs

    Cutting

    RPM elevate chicken & meat cutting to an art form. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, our skilled butchers master the craft of meat preparation, ensuring every cut meets the highest standards of quality and precision.

    Cold Storage

    RPM specialize in cutting-edge cold storage solutions designed to keep our products in perfect condition. Our state-of-the-art facilities and advanced refrigeration technology ensure that our goods are stored at optimal temperatures, preserving their quality and extending their shelf life.

    Dry Storage

    From retail goods and industrial products to archival records and personal belongings, RPM offers reliable dry storage solutions that help us manage our inventory effectively. Provide us the secure and dependable storage space our goods deserve.

    Delivery

    We pride ourselves on providing top-notch delivery solutions that ensure our products reach their destination safely, on time, and in perfect condition. Our comprehensive delivery services are designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

    After Sales

    RPM dedication to customer satisfaction doesn’t end with the purchase. Our comprehensive after-sales services is designed to provide ongoing support and ensure that you get the most out of your investment. We’re here to assist you every step of the way.
